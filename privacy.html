Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

At Watermelon AI, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Sentinel, our productivity assistant service.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address and contact information
  • Calendar data and scheduling preferences
  • Task and productivity data
  • Communication preferences

Usage Information

  • How you interact with our service
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Performance and analytics data
  • Device and browser information

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our productivity services
  • Personalize your experience with smart recommendations
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our features
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we regularly review our security practices to ensure your information remains safe.

Third-Party Integrations

Sentinel integrates with various third-party services (Google Calendar, Slack, Outlook, etc.) to provide seamless productivity features. We only access the minimum data necessary to provide these integrations and follow each platform's privacy guidelines.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You can request deletion of your data at any time.
