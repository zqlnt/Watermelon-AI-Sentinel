Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 August 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Sentinel ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our application and related services. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information – name, email address, and authentication credentials (OAuth tokens for email/calendar integration).
  • Connected Service Data – emails, contacts, events, and tasks from linked accounts (e.g., Gmail, Google Calendar), processed only with your permission.
  • Usage Data – actions taken within the app, device type, IP address, and browser information.
  • Support Communications – messages or emails you send to our support team.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core features (email search, dashboard integration, calendar management).
  • Sync and process your data from connected services.
  • Improve performance and user experience.
  • Send important updates and respond to support requests.
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse of the service.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Your raw email and calendar content is stored securely and may be encrypted end‑to‑end where possible.
  • Email embeddings (mathematical representations for AI features) are stored separately to protect content confidentiality.
  • We use secure servers located in the UK and/or EEA.
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored only to maintain your active connections.

4. Data Sharing

We only share your data with:

  • Service providers – e.g., cloud hosting, AI processing, and analytics platforms, all bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal authorities – only when required by law.

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access the data we hold about you, request corrections, request deletion ("right to be forgotten"), withdraw consent, and receive a copy of your data in a portable format.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at office@watermelonai.com.

6. Cookies

Cookies are not currently in use

7. Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. If you delete your account, we will remove associated data within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified via the app or email.
